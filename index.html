Beauty Studio FAQs

Quick answers for clients comparing nail art, bridal beauty, home service and academy training in Jaipur.

Where is the studio?

Tha Nail Art Shop By Pratibha™ is in Vaishali Nagar, Jaipur, near Burger King at Amrapali Circle.

Do you offer home service?

Yes. Premium home service is available in Jaipur and across India for selected nail, bridal and beauty bookings.

What services can I book?

You can book nail art, gel nails, acrylic or polygel extensions, bridal nails, hair extensions, lashes, PMU, hydra facial and skin care.

Do you teach professional courses?

Yes. The academy offers hands-on courses in nail art, hair extensions, microblading, permanent makeup and skin care.
